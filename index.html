About the Artist

Hi, I'm Ritu Verma

Passionate face & body artist dedicated to turning skin into stunning works of art.

From delicate floral designs for bridal events to wild fantasy transformations for festivals — I bring every vision to life with safe, skin-friendly paints and a steady hand. My work has graced weddings, corporate events, school fairs, and everything in between.

I believe every face tells a story. Let me help tell yours.
